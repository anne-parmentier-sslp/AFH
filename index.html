Silent Auction

Must be present to win.

Buy Now option available. You may purchase any silent auction item on the spot for its fair market value. See volunteers in the saloon for details.

Schedule: Bidding opens at 12:00 PM, last call at 2:25 PM, auction closes at 2:30 PM. Winners announced at 2:40 PM.

Minimum bid increments are listed on each bid sheet. Please write legibly and include a phone number.

All items are sold “as-is.” No exchanges or refunds. Accepted payments: cash and credit/debit. Checks payable to Adoptive Families of Houston.
